choatelabs.app / voltanode

Project 04 · Web · Running live

A bench full of bots, trading the topology.

VoltaNode is a multi-strategy paper-trading platform — a bench of fifteen strategy bots, from momentum and MACD to mean-reversion, news-sentiment and a permissive trend-follow, ride 24/7 crypto and market-hours stocks through Alpaca, while an LLM advisor stitches fundamentals, technicals, macro, and recent catalysts into a single thesis.

Python & FastAPI · React & Vite · Alpaca paper · Live

What's on the bench

⏚

Multi-strategy engine

Momentum, MACD, mean-reversion, breakout, news-sentiment, ensemble-ML, and a permissive trend-follow bot — each running independently on its own ticker set.

⌬

Crypto 24/7, stocks 9:30–16:00 ET

The engine gates equity strategies to US-market hours and lets crypto strategies tick around the clock. No wasted broker calls when the floor is closed.

⊜

LLM research advisor

Claude Haiku for the fast lane, 1T-parameter free-tier models for the heavy lane. Reports fold fundamentals, technicals, sentiment, FRED macro, insider Form 4s, and last week's headlines into one Investment Thesis.

◷

Per-bar latch

Strategies are evaluated every 5 seconds; bars roll daily. The latch keeps each strategy from re-firing the same crossover hundreds of times within a single bar.

⇩

SELL clamp + dust skip

If a bot signals a SELL larger than the held position, the engine clamps to what's actually owned. Sub-$10 dust gets a single rejection and goes silent for an hour.

⛌

Order debounce

Five-minute cooldown per (strategy, symbol, side) on rejected orders. One-hour suppression for structurally impossible orders. The broker sees signal, never spam.

▤

Honest dashboard

Real allocation pie from real positions. Em-dashes for stats that need closed trades. No cosmetic fake equity curve climbing while you wait for the first round-trip.

◉

Macro signals strip

Fear & Greed, VIX, Fed Funds, 10Y, 10Y–2Y spread — pulled from FRED + alternative.me on the dashboard, also fed back into the bots as gates.

Built to not fool itself

⊗

Circuit breakers

Equity-aware exposure, position-size, daily-loss and order-rate limits — checked before every order, re-checked after each fill. A latched kill switch halts the whole engine, not one bot.

⊨

Backtest that can't flatter itself

Equity is cash plus signed market value. Sells only deposit on a real exit, buys cap to available cash, stops and targets actually fire. Hard to fool.

⊘

Out-of-sample promotion gate

Combinatorial purged cross-validation and a Deflated Sharpe Ratio decide what gets promoted — so a strategy has to clear out-of-sample bars, not just curve-fit the past.

⟲

Searchable trade memory

Every closed trade is logged with its setup and outcome to a searchable memory. The worst-performing patterns surface first, so the next decision learns from the last.

The strategy roster.

simple_trendPermissive workhorse — buys when price is above both EMAs, sells when below. One trade per signal flip per bar.
momentumClassic 12/26 EMA crossover with a 200-EMA trend filter. Fires once per fresh golden / death cross.
mean_reversionRSI & Bollinger Band touch — buys oversold dips into the lower band, sells overbought touches at the upper.
macdMACD line crossing the signal line with histogram confirmation. Both directions, per-bar latched.
news_sentimentVADER local + LLM hybrid scoring on news headlines. Trades sharp shifts in tone with a trend confirmation.
breakoutMulti-timeframe channel breakouts. Buys new highs, shorts new lows on confirmed range exits.
arbitrageScaffold for cross-venue spread trading. Runs against a single synthetic counter-market — no real second venue is wired yet — so it's a structural placeholder, not a live edge.
ensemble_mlAggregates the other strategies' signals through a weighted scorer. The committee vote layer.

Eight of fifteen registered strategies. The rest — multi_coin, auto_discovery, squeeze, funding_carry, bband_rsi, swing_high_to_sky, grid — round out the bench.

Built on

Boring stack, careful glue.

FastAPI for the backend, React + Vite for the dashboard, Alpaca for the broker, yfinance & CoinGecko & CoinMarketCap as the price feeds, FRED & Finnhub & alternative.me for the signals, OpenRouter for the LLM advisor.
