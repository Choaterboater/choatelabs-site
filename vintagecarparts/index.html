choatelabs.app / vintagecarparts

Project 06 · iOS · In development

Scan any vintage part. Know it. Fit it. Price it.

VintageCarParts turns your iPhone into the swap-meet companion — point the camera at a mystery part, decode the casting number, check it against the cars in your garage, and see what it's really worth. Before the seller names a price.

iPhone · iOS 16+ · Coming Soon

Coming Soon to the App Store See Features

Capabilities

◉

Point-and-scan identification

Aim the camera and the on-device classifier matches the part against the bundled catalog in real time. No typing, no guessing, no "I'll look it up at home."

⌗

Casting-number decode

Reads Ford, GM, and Mopar casting numbers straight off the part — C6AE-6015-A, 7028207, 3927184 — and tells you family, era, and application. Manual lookup included for the parts you already pulled.

✦

Expert Scan

When the part is rare, rough, or just weird, run the deeper AI read: identification, era, application list, and honest condition notes from the photo you choose.

⊞

216-part reference catalog

A browsable, searchable catalog across carburetion, ignition, brakes, suspension, electrical, and more — every part with its own studio reference image.

⌖

Garage & fitment

Add your cars — VIN decodes automatically via the public NHTSA API — and get a fit verdict on the spot: does this part belong on your build, or someone else's?

⇄

Real sold prices

One tap from an identified part to eBay's completed-sale listings. Not asking prices — what people actually paid.

⧉

Ready-to-post listings

Selling instead of buying? Export a scan as a ready-to-post listing — title, specs, condition — copied to your clipboard in one tap.

▤

My Scans

Every scan saved on-device with its photo, result, and your notes. The swap-meet haul, documented before it hits the shelf.

Your garage is your business.

Private by default.

Live scanning and casting OCR run entirely on-device. Expert Scan sends only the photo you choose to the AI service — never your identity, location, or anything else. Scan history and your garage stay on your phone.
