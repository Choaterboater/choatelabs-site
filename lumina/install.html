Project 02 · Web & iOS

LUMINA

Neon Orbit Arcade

One-touch neon arcade survival. Hold to flip your orbit, dodge geometry, chase Perfects for triple points. Unleash Burst to clear the field, climb the Mastery ranks, and post your score to the global leaderboard — even when you're offline.

▶ Play in browser

What's in the game

One-Touch

Tap and hold to reverse your orbit. Let go to flip back. That's it.

Perfect Hits

Flip the instant a coin reaches your orb — score 3× points and hear the chime.

Burst Power-Up

Charge it on Perfects, unleash a shockwave that wipes the field and lights up the board.

Mastery Ranks

Eight tiers from Initiate to Luminary. Every badge you earn moves the dial.

Daily Challenge

14 rotating modifiers — Speed Demon, Glass Cannon, Swarm, Coin Rush, and more.

Global Leaderboard

Pick a nickname, climb the ranks. Your run, your name, your spot.

Plays Offline

No signal, no problem. Scores queue and submit themselves the moment you're back online.

Unlock Everything

6 orb skins with unique perks, 4 trails, 5 upgrades, 24 badges, 4 power-ups.

No Ads, No IAP

No tracking, no paywalls, no purchases. Just pure arcade survival.
