LUMINA — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

LUMINA: Neon Orbit does not collect personal information. This page explains exactly what we do (and don't do) with data.

What we store on your device

  • Game progress: best score, coins, unlocked skins, lifetime stats, settings.
  • A randomly generated player ID (UUID) used to deduplicate leaderboard entries.
  • Your chosen leaderboard nickname (1–16 characters of A–Z, 0–9, spaces).

This data lives on your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

What we send to our server

When and only when you submit a score to the leaderboard, we send:

  • The player ID and your chosen nickname.
  • The score, your max combo for that run, run duration, number of Perfects.

Your IP address is hashed (SHA-256) for rate-limit purposes only. The hash is not stored in clear form, not correlated with the score, and is purged on a rolling basis.

Game Center (iOS only)

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple shares your Game Center identifier with the app for the purpose of submitting scores and achievements. Apple's Game Center terms apply. We do not store the Game Center identifier on our server.

What we don't do

  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or third-party tracking.
  • We do not collect contact info, location, contacts, photos, financial info, health data, browsing history, or any sensitive information.
  • We do not sell or share your data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Children

LUMINA is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
