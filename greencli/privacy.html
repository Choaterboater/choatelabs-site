Privacy Policy

GreenCli — Choate Labs · Effective June 2026

Short version: GreenCli stores everything on your Mac. Nothing is sent to Choate Labs. Ever.

What we collect

Nothing. Choate Labs collects no data from GreenCli. There are no analytics, no crash reporters, and no usage metrics that phone home to us.

What the app stores locally

GreenCli stores the following data on your machine only:

  • Session credentials (hostnames, usernames, passwords, SSH keys) — encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM + Argon2
  • Session metadata (folders, tags, connection history)
  • App settings and preferences (stored in macOS app data directory)
  • SSH known hosts cache
  • AI API keys you configure — stored locally, never sent to Choate Labs

AI providers

If you use the AI assistant, GreenCli sends your prompts and (optionally) terminal output directly from your Mac to your chosen AI provider — Anthropic, an OpenAI-compatible endpoint, or a local Ollama instance. Choate Labs is never in that path. Review your chosen provider's privacy policy for how they handle requests.

MCP servers

If you connect to external MCP servers (such as centralmcp for Aruba Central), data flows directly between your Mac and that server. Choate Labs is not involved. You configure and trust those connections yourself.

Network connections

GreenCli makes outbound connections only to:

  • Hosts you explicitly connect to (SSH, Telnet, Serial)
  • AI API endpoints you configure
  • MCP servers you configure
  • REST API endpoints you configure in the API explorer

It does not contact Choate Labs servers, DNS, or any update service.

Third-party SDKs

GreenCli includes no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
