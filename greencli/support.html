Frequently asked questions

What platforms does GreenCli run on?

GreenCli is a macOS desktop app built with Tauri. Windows and Linux builds are on the roadmap.

What vendors are supported?

Current syntax highlighting and auto-detection supports:

  • Aruba CX Switches (AOS-CX)
  • Aruba AOS-S Switches
  • Aruba Wireless APs
  • Aruba Mobility Controllers (AOS-8)
  • Juniper JunOS
  • Mist
  • Generic SSH / Telnet / Serial
How does the AI assistant work?

GreenCli's AI panel connects directly from your Mac to your chosen provider — Anthropic Claude, any OpenAI-compatible endpoint (OpenRouter, Moonshot, LiteLLM, etc.), or a local Ollama instance. Your API key is stored in the local app settings. Choate Labs is never in the request path.

What is MCP and how does GreenCli use it?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) lets the AI assistant call live tools — like querying Aruba Central for device state, NAC policies, or monitoring data. GreenCli acts as an MCP client and connects to servers you configure, such as centralmcp. You control which servers the app connects to.

Are my credentials safe?

Yes. Passwords and SSH keys are stored in a local vault encrypted with AES-256-GCM, key-derived with Argon2. Nothing is synced to the cloud or sent to Choate Labs. See the privacy policy for full details.
